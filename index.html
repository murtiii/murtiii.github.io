Poor Founders

'Coz life ain't gonna wait for your IPO

A Fund that buys Secondary shares from founders & early-employees whose companies are post-product-market fit and absolutely crushing. These founders are typically rich on paper, but poor IRL and need some liquidity.

Back Story

We did a secondary round 4 years before our last company Streamlabs was sold. Back then (2015), founder secondaries were not common, and the only reason we got a chance was that we had an acquisition offer on the table and the VCs were trying to bribe us to reject it. That liquidity eased financial pressures on us and allowed us to go much further and longer with Streamlabs. We hope to give other founders this option.

From a selfish POV, we feel this is a great assest class that can generate sustained returns over many years.

Founders & Early Employees

You are the founder or early stage employee and your company is absolutely crushing it. And yet, life doesn't wait. You just got engaged, or want to start a family. Wouldn't it be nice to have some funds to buy a house? Whats the use of being a millionare on paper if you have to wait 10 years? We can help. Consider selling us a small part of your equity. You still keep almost all the upside and get some cash now to get on with life.
